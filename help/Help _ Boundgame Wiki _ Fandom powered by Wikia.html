BATTLE Edit

Attack Edit

In Bound, the most standard way to attack an enemy in battle, is to use the attack button. The attack button functions as a regular attack, and will get stronger the more a character levels up. Certain classes have higher "Attack" stats then other classes. This can be checked in the Main Menu (outside of battle), under the "Status" page. You can also increase a party member's basic attack by equipping weapons to them. You can also power up a party member's attack by giving them "Attack stat" boosting armor, or by using an item that boosts a character's attack.

Guarding Edit

in Bound, the Guard button can be used in battle to reduce incoming damage. The guard button can also be used to completely evade/dodge attacks. The guard function also has other uses as well. The guard function can be used to block status effects as well. If one of your party members guards an attack that gives a negative status effect, it will block the effect as well. These status ailments include:

  • Poison
  • Blind
  • Silence
  • Confusion
  • Sleep
  • Paralyze

However, there is an element of risk when blocking. The actual success rate using the guard function is 70%. There is still a 30% chance that your guard will fail. If your guard fails, your character will be unable to make any other moves until his/her next turn.

Skills Edit

In Bound, you can use "Skills". There are various different skills, with a variety of different effects. Thre are 2 types of skills: "ESP" skills, and "Special" skills.

ESP Edit

ESP skills can only be used by characters who have "ESP" as their class. ESP skills can be used in battle by selecting them on the battle screen. The ESP button appears above the guard function. To use ESP skills, you must have the sufficient amount of MP. You can check your MP by looking at the blue bar on your characters profile. Different ESP skills have different MP costs. Certain ESP skills can also use TP, You can see your TP bar beside your MP bar. Your TP bar is the green one.

Special Edit

Special skills can be used by characters of any class. Special skills can be used in battle by selecting them on the battle screen. The Special button appears above the guard function. To use Special skills, you must have the sufficient amount of MP. You can check your MP by looking at the blue bar on your characters profile. Different Special skills have different MP costs. Certain Special skills can also use TP, You can see your TP bar beside your MP bar. Your TP bar is the green one.

Turn Order Edit

In Bound, all battles are turn based. You and your party take turns attacking against the enemy. The turn order is displayed in the top right screen while battling. Icons that have blue outlines are allies, and icons that have red icons are enemies. By using the turn order bar, you can plan ahead knowing when each member of your party will attack.
